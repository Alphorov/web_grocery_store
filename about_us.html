Our Story

Founded in 2003 by the Johnson family, FreshMart began as a small corner store with a simple mission: to provide our neighbors with the freshest, highest-quality groceries at fair prices.

Over the years, we've grown from a single location to serving thousands of families across the region, but our commitment to quality and community has never wavered.

Today, we work directly with over 50 local farms and suppliers, ensuring that our shelves are stocked with the freshest seasonal produce and artisanal products our region has to offer.
